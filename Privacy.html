Privacy Policy

Daily Tools Hub aapki privacy ka khayal rakhta hai. Neeche bataya gaya hai ke ham data ko kaise handle karte hain:

Information Collection

  • Ham aapse koi personally identifiable information automatically collect nahi karte.
  • Hamare tools (Age, BMI, Converters) ki calculations aapke browser me hoti hain—server par store nahi hoti.

Cookies & Analytics

  • Site performance improve karne ke liye basic analytics use ho sakta hai (aggregated, non-identifiable).
  • Agar aap third-party ads (jaise Google AdSense) enable karte ho, to unki cookies policy un par apply hogi.

Third-Party Ads

  • Ads providers cookies ka use kar sakte hain personalized ads ke liye. Users browser settings se cookies manage kar sakte hain.

Data Rights

  • Agar aapko apne data, feedback ya deletion request ke bare me baat karni ho, to hume email karein: support@dailytoolshub.com

Policy Updates

  • Hum is policy ko update kar sakte hain. Latest version hamesha is page par available hoga.
